## 14-day withdrawal right (EU consumers)

If you are an EU consumer buying at a distance, you may generally withdraw from your purchase within 14 days without giving a reason.

The withdrawal period starts from the day you or a third party indicated by you receives the goods.

## How to exercise withdrawal

Send a clear statement by email to {{ legal.contact_email }} before the withdrawal period ends. Include:

- Your name
- Order details
- Date of receipt
- A clear statement that you wish to withdraw

## Return shipping and refunds

- You must return goods without undue delay and no later than 14 days after communicating withdrawal.
- Unless otherwise agreed, direct return shipping costs are borne by the customer.
- Refunds are made within 14 days after we receive the returned goods or proof of return, using the original payment method when possible.

## Legal exceptions

The right of withdrawal may not apply to certain goods, including sealed goods not suitable for return for health protection or hygiene reasons if unsealed after delivery, and other exceptions under applicable law.

## Damaged or incorrect items

If you receive a damaged or incorrect item, contact {{ legal.contact_email }} as soon as possible with order details and photos if available.

## Model withdrawal form

Ordered on: [date]
Received on: [date]
Name: [name]
